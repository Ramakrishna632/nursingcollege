Admission & Selection Process

Admissions are open for the academic year 2026. We provide complete guidance for Nursing and Paramedical courses.

Step 3: Apply for Admission

Fill the admission form online or contact us through Call or WhatsApp.

Submit required documents and confirm your seat. Limited seats available.
